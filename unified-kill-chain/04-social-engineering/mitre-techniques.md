# MITRE ATT&CK Techniques - Social Engineering

## ⚠️ Note: Unified Kill Chain Specific Phase
**Social Engineering** is a phase specific to the Unified Kill Chain framework. While it maps to MITRE ATT&CK's **Initial Access (TA0001)** tactic, "Social Engineering" focuses specifically on the manipulation of people to perform unsafe actions. This phase emphasizes human psychology and social manipulation techniques, which may be part of broader initial access methods in MITRE ATT&CK.

## Overview
This document lists all MITRE ATT&CK techniques relevant to the Social Engineering phase (Phase 4).
